N64 Logo

This one took lots of time, over 5 hours in fact. I have always wanted a 3D Nintendo 64 logo. I only have one extruder, and there was a design which you could print with different filaments in series and then assemble. I don’t have each of the colours though, but this would be interesting. Because each piece was face down, then all faces would be nide and smooth.

New filament

Starting on the green stuff! It’s just what was cheap on Amazon, I think if I was making a model I would sand and prime anyway.

Printing

There are 90 degree corners on this one, and I’m not using glue on he bed any more. I’ll add a brim to prevent curling,a nd I can peel it off afterwards.

I got sone slight elephant feet on the pillars. 60 degrees on the bed must have been slightly too hot.
