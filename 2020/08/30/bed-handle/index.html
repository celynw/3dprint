Bed Handle

It’s pretty difficult to move the bed around without touching the bed plate. This is sometimes hot, and I also don’t want to change the levelling. Luckily there are many solutions on Thingiverse for the Ender 3, which will be compatible with my Ender 3 V2. I settled on one which was easier to print without supports, and also featured bed screw locking clips.

Everything printed fine (but I printed an extra part because I forgot to flip it…) Nice to see the cool gyroid infill pattern at a larger scale!

The handle is flimsier than I predicted, and doesn’t leave enough room to mount the webcam with my tripod. This is OK, because Octolapse provides some great features and I don’t need to couple the camera and bed motion. The clips are awfl as well. I think the intention is to glue them in for some reason, and they just keep falling out. The shape doesn’t account for the different relative directions of the screws either. At least the bed handle will come in useful.
