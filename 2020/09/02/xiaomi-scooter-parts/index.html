Xiaomi Scooter Parts

A friend wanted a replacement part for his Xiaomi M365 scooter. Of course there are lots of these on Thingiverse. Settled on an extra shopping bag hook.

I tried to print with the bed at 45 degrees, didn’t stick well at all. I raised it back to 50 and cleaned the bed - much better. To avoid elephant feet, is there a way I can reduce it after the first layers have adhered? Yes, but only after first layer (maybe that means the floor, so first layers).

I’m going for some overhang here with no supports… mainly because I didn’t need support in both parts It should end up fine because it’s a bridge, but maybe a layer or two could be messy. I also tightened the X and Y axes after what happened with the second Nintendo 64 logo. Since these parts are to be strong, I used thicker walls with 40% infill and some other features.

The bridge went OK! I got some curling on one sharp end of the hook, damn. I want to use different settings for different models, but print them at the same time. I would have used supports on one, and a brim on the other. Or maybe a brim only on sharp corners? Maybe there’s a way to have a brim easier to remove, or see to remove?

Bring on Cura plugins! There’s one where you can set manual brims with a mouse click, I’ll use this in future!
