N64 Logo Version 2

Well, the last attempt was a waste of time. Note to self: Do not expect things like this to go together!

I used a very old glue gun, which made things worse. But it was very difficult to find the brimm boundaries, assemble, and keep together. Just print a solid one.

No brim this time, 90 degrees seems fine most of the time, especially with good vertical height and repeated layers.

I got very slight elephant feet at 50 degrees, perhaps I can go down to 45? Also the layers aren’t completely flush. I guess I should tighten my belts. Since this model doesn’t have a ‘back’, I tried to hide the seams randomly. They don’t look particularly random on the print. I guess this one is easy to sand, though.
